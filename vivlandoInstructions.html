03

Turn on shaders

The pretty stuff ✧ — get everything sparkly in a couple of clicks.

Step 1

Open the shader folder

In game, go to Options → Video Settings → Shaders, then click Shaders Folder to open it.

🖼️
shader1.png
Video Settings → Shaders → Shaders Folder
Step 2

Find some shader packs

Browse Iris shader packs here: modrinth.com Iris shaders ✧

🖼️
shader2.png
Browsing Iris shaders on Modrinth
Step 3

Download a shader pack

Download the shader you like — choose the latest version and pick Iris.

🖼️
shader3.png
Latest version → Iris → download
Step 4

Drop in the shader pack

Move the shader pack file into that folder, then head back to the Shaders menu in game.

🖼️
shader4.png
Shader pack inside the folder
Step 5

Select it & enjoy ✧

Click the shader pack in the list to enable it. Give it a sec to load — and welcome to the pretty world ♡
